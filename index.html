Game of Life w/ Cellular Age

The grid is Grid Size x Grid Size cells large
"Life Rule" determines whether a living cell will be
dead in the next iteration depending on how many
cells around it are alive
"Death Rule" determines whether a dead cell will be
living in the next iteration depending on how
many cells around it are alive
Cells always die after Max Age years living
You can edit these sliders in real time
(Besides Grid Size, that will reset the grid)

Press Enter to Begin/Restart Simulation
